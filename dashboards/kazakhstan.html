Political Timeline
1991
Kazakhstan declares independence from USSR on Dec 16. Nursultan Nazarbayev becomes first president.
1997
Capital relocated from Almaty to Akmola (later renamed Astana). Ambitious nation-building project begins.
2019
After 29 years, Nazarbayev resigns. Tokayev elected president.
2022
Nationwide unrest (Bloody January). Russian troops deployed briefly. Constitutional reforms introduced.
2024
Voters approve construction of first nuclear power plant. GDP growth accelerates to 6%+.
